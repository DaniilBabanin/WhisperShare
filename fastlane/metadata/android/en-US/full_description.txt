WhisperShare turns voice messages into text without sending a single byte to a server. Long-press a voice note in WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, or any voice recorder, hit Share, pick WhisperShare, and the transcript appears a moment later — ready to copy, share, or paste into a reply.

Everything runs locally on your phone using whisper.cpp, the open-source C++ port of OpenAI's Whisper model. Audio never leaves the device. The only network traffic the app makes is the optional, opt-in download of a transcription model from huggingface.co the first time you tap the cloud icon next to a model.

Highlights:

• Local only. No cloud, no analytics, no telemetry. Works in airplane mode after the model is downloaded.
• Share-sheet integration. Long-press → Share → WhisperShare. No copy-paste, no manual export.
• Multilingual. Transcribe German, English, French, Spanish, Russian, and 95+ other languages, or let Whisper auto-detect.
• Translate to English. Optional flag to translate non-English audio directly into English text.
• Multiple model sizes. Choose Tiny (31 MB, fastest) for clear speech, Base (60 MB, recommended) as a balanced default, Small (190 MB, most accurate) for accents or noisy recordings, or English-only Base for slightly faster pure-English transcription.
• GPU acceleration on capable devices via whisper.cpp's Vulkan backend. Falls back to CPU automatically.
• arm64-v8a Android 12 or newer.

The app contains no ads, no tracking SDKs, no crash reporters. Source code is MIT-licensed and available on GitHub.

How it works:

When you share an audio file, WhisperShare decodes it via Android's MediaCodec into 16 kHz mono PCM, hands it to whisper.cpp through a JNI bridge, and runs whisper_full() in a coroutine. The decoded audio and the transcript stay in app-private memory and are discarded as soon as you close the screen.

Models are stored under the app's private data directory and can be deleted at any time from the home screen.

Permissions used:

• INTERNET — only to download Whisper models you explicitly request.
• POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to surface transcription progress on long files.

Designed for Pixel 9 / Tensor G4 but runs on any modern arm64 Android device.
